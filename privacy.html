FitCheckAI Privacy Policy

FitCheckAI analyzes outfit photos to provide style feedback.

Photos you submit are securely transmitted to our servers for processing. Images are used only to generate analysis results and are not sold or shared with third parties.

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or location unless explicitly provided by the user.

Photos may be temporarily processed by AI services to generate feedback but are not stored permanently.

FitCheckAI does not store user photos after analysis.

FitCheckAI does not use submitted photos to train AI models.
